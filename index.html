Half-Day Tours

Athens Highlights Mythology Tour (4 hours)

Discover the myths behind the greatest landmarks in Athens! Enjoy a unique 4-hour mythological tour that will help you understand how Athens became one of the greatest cities in the world and a beacon of civilization. Our expert guide will let you know all about Greek gods and heroes in an entertaining way and will help you discover what really hides behind the universal myths! At the same time, you’ll get to visit the most impressive landmarks and sites in Athens: The majestic Acropolis (the Parthenon, the Theater of Dionysus, the Temple of Athena Nike, The Erechtheion, the Sanctuary of Asclepius), the Temple of Olympian Zeus, the Ancient Agora, and the temple of Hephaestus! A fun and lively experience that will give you real insight.

Beat the Crowds Afternoon Acropolis Tour (2 hours)

The afternoon hours are the best time of the day to visit the greatest landmark in Athens. As the crowds have thinned and the summer heat has eased, you’ll visit the world-famous Acropolis in ideal conditions. Learn about Greek gods and heroes from your top-rated licensed guide and see the universal monuments on the Acropolis hill up close in this 2-hour afternoon tour.

The Delicious Athens Food Tour (3.5 hours)

During this tour we will discover Greek flavors and their history with an easy-paced stroll walk through some of the city’s most interesting and less touristy neighborhoods. We will visit food markets and carefully selected deli stores and we will help you familiarize yourselves with the most famous flavors of the Greek cuisine, alongside a top-rated guide! Our tastings include olive oils, tapenades, rusks, tsipouro drink, cheeses, Greek souvlaki, a variety of Greek tapas and sweets (bougatsa, baklava, Greek donuts).

Unexpected Athens Orientation Tour (4 hours)

In this tour we will experience the real Athens, as it has evolved through the centuries. Specifically, we will visit 5 contrasting neighborhoods and understand how 4 different cities coexist and intertwine: the ancient, the Ottoman, the neoclassical, and modern Athens, with glimpses into Athens of World War II. It lasts 4 hours, starts from Parliament Square, and ends in the Monastiraki area, covering also Kolonaki, Exarchia, Syntagma, Psiri, and the Athens Trilogy.

The Olympic Games Workout (2 hours)

A fun and easy outdoor experience capturing the true essence of the Olympics, set in venues tied to authentic Olympic events such as Zappeion and the Panathenaic Stadium. Discover the true Olympic spirit and practice your skills in long jump, shot put, javelin throw, and tug of war, culminating in a race at the world’s first modern Olympic Stadium, like a true ancient Greek Olympian athlete!

Cape Sounio & Athens Riviera Sunset Tour (5 hours)

An impressive side of Athens and a scenic highlight no doubt, the Athens Riviera has it all. We’ll see the impressive Niarchos Cultural Center and the natural wonder setting of Lake Vouliagmeni. We’ll discover real life along the city’s coastline, visit a ‘hidden’ ancient temple, and visit the temple at Sounio as the sun sets.
